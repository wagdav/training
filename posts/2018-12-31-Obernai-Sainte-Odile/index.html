Obernai-Mont Sainte-Odlie

Posted on December 31, 2018 hike

End of the year hike in Alsace. Start from Obernai towards Bernardswiller and Saint Nabor. There, a steep climb starts to the monastery of Mont Sainte-Odile. We take a short break on the top then head to Otrott, finishing in back in Obernai.
