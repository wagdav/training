Nyon - Chalet de la Dôle - St. Cergue

From Nyon follow the hiking trail No. 5 (Sentier des crêtes). First 7 km is flat/slighly up, then steep clim to the Chalet de la Dôle. The last couple of kilometers down to St. Cergue is easy.
