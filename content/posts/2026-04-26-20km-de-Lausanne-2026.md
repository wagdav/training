# Preparation

The third consecutive year I followed a 16 week long, self-guided, heart rate
based training plan from [Garmin Connect](https://connect.garmin.com).

Already at the second week I skipped a few training days of the flu but I doubt
that this had any effect of the later weeks.  Unfortunately, the flu I caught
just a week before the race certainly cost me some seconds on my final time.

The test runs at week 8 and week 13 both showed that I could not realistically
beat my last year's time.  I ran them both 1 minute slower than last year.  I
guess if I lose 1 minute on 10km losing 2 minutes on 20km makes sense.
