Ardèche Tour 2024

Mon 17 June 2024 David Wagner

The tour starts and ends in Irigny, a few km south from Lyon. On the first day we ride through the Parc naturel régional du Pilat to Saint-Félicien where we join the Gorges - Ardèche tour recommended by the local tourist office.

In fact, I started biking four days before the tour starts. I cycled from Lausanne to Irigny in two days.
