Heitenriederlauf 2024

Sat 06 April 2024 David Wagner

10.2 km race Heitenried as preparation for this year's 20 km de Lausanne. The race is on gravel and asphalt roads with approximately 180m of elevation gain.

I was 16th at the finish line (6th in my age category) with a time 40:57. This corresponds to roughly 4 min/km pace which is my best result at a race with such altitude profile.
