Argentario (Tuscany)

Fri 30 August 2019 David Wagner

GPX
Distance
26km

Hike around the Monte Argentario peninsula in Tuscany. Starting and finishing in Porto Santo Stefano. We visited two beaces: Cala del Gesso and Sassi Verdi. It's very hot and we have to walk a lot on asphalt.
