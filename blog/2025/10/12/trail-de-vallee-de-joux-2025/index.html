Trail de Vallée de Joux 2025

Sun 12 October 2025 David Wagner

GPX
Time
02:11:02
Distance
22km
Elevation gain

I close the running season with Trail de Vallée de Joux, which I run the third time today. I felt untrained for running on the trains. I arrived pretty cooked at the finish line with a time much slower than in 2019 and 2018.

Perfect weather, beautiful sunshine on the Mont Tendre and in the finish line.

During the first hour the climb feels hard, but I climb steadily. On the way down my legs feel heavy and tired, I fall twice, so I just take it easy.
